Faculties
Faculty of Science

Faculty departments are accredited by FEDEK. In the departments of Chemistry and Statistics, 30% of the courses are in English, while in the other departments the language of instruction is 100% Turkish. There is compulsory English preparatory education in Chemistry and Statistics departments. It is optional in Physics, Biology and Mathematics Departments. Internship in Biology, Physics and Statistics departments is compulsory, while in other departments it is optional.

  • Department of Statistics
  • Department of Physics
  • Department of Chemistry
  • Department of Biology
  • Department of Mathematics
Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences

It was established in 1986 as 'Civil Aviation Vocational School'. It was renamed 'Civil Aviation College' in 1992. In 2012, it was renamed as 'Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences'. Eskişehir Technical University has its own airport.

  • Pilotage Department
  • Department of Aviation Electrical Electronics
  • Airframe-Engine Maintenance Department
  • Department of Aviation Management
  • Air Traffic Control Division
Faculty of Architecture and Design

Architecture department was separated from the Faculty of Engineering and Architecture. It was established in 2011 as "Faculty of Architecture and Design".

  • Department of Architecture

  • Industrial Design Department
Faculty of Engineering

It was established in 1970 as "Eskişehir State Engineering-Architecture Academy". In 1983, it was named "Faculty of Engineering and Architecture". In 1993, it was transferred to Osmangazi University with the infrastructure, machinery and equipment of the faculty. It was renewed in 1994 and started education again. In 2012, the department of architecture within the faculty was separated and the name of the faculty was changed to "Faculty of Engineering". Located in the Faculty of Engineering İki Eylül Campus, the education language of the faculty is 30% English and 100% English. 1 year English preparatory education is given and its departments are accredited by MÜDEK.

  • Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (English)
  • Department of Computer Engineering (English)
  • Department of Industrial Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering Department
  • Department of Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering Department
  • Department of Materials Science and Engineering (English)
  • Mechanical Engineering Department

Institute of Transport Sciences

It was established in 1993 as "Transportation Economics Research Institute". In 2012, it was named "Institute of Transportation Sciences". It is the first and only institute working in this field.

Institute of Earth and Space Sciences

In 1989, Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems studies started. It was established in 1993 as "Satellite and Space Sciences Research Institute". In 2012, it was named "Institute of Earth and Space Sciences".

  • Department of Geodesy and Geographic Information Technologies
  • Department of Satellite and Space Sciences
  • Department of Earth Sciences and Earthquake Engineering
